Suzuki Jimny 1.5 GLX MT

Experience unmatched performance with key capabilities that define serious off-roading. With its rigid ladder frame chassis, 3-link rigid axle suspension, and angled body with impressive ground clearance, the Jimny is a conqueror. It's equipped with 4WD and low-range transfer gear, powered by a purposeful 1.5ℓ engine, and enhanced with traction and hill descent control - engineered to adventure beyond limits.

Hill Hold Control: Helps prevent rolling back on inclines.
