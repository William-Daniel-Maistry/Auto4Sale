Key Features and Specifications

Engine: 2.0L Bi-Turbo diesel engine producing approximately 157 kW and 500 Nm of torque, ensuring excellent power for off-road adventures.

Transmission: 10-speed automatic transmission for smooth and efficient gear shifting.

Fuel Efficiency: Estimated 8.0 liters per 100 km , offering a balance between power and fuel economy for both city and off-road driving.

Drive Type: Full-time 4WD (Four-Wheel Drive) for enhanced off-road capability and traction on all surfaces.

Cargo Space: 898 liters (with seats up) - Generous boot space with a capacity of up to 1,000 liters when seats are folded, ideal for family trips or carrying gear.

Safety Features

Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags for comprehensive protection during travel

ABS: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) for safe, controlled braking.

EBD: Electronic Brakeforce Distribution for optimized braking performance across all conditions.

Lane Assist: Lane Keeping Assist to help keep you safely on course.

Collision Prevention: Adaptive Cruise Control and Forward Collision Warning to reduce the risk of accidents.

Parking Sensors: Front and rear parking sensors for easy parking, even in tight spots.

Infotainment System: 8-inch SYNC 3 touchscreen with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and navigation for seamless connectivity and entertainment.

Climate Control: Dual-zone automatic air conditioning for ultimate comfort on all drives.

Seating: 7 seats - Leather upholstery with power-adjustable seats and memory function for the driver's seat.

Storage: Practical interior storage compartments, cup holders, and an organized center console for added convenience.
