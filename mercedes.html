Key Features and Specifications

Engine: 1.3L 4-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine producing around 120 kW and 250 Nm of torque.

Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

Fuel Efficiency: Estimated 6.0 liters per 100 km, providing a balance of power and fuel economy.

Drive Type: Front-wheel drive (FWD) for efficient handling.

Cargo Space: Spacious trunk with 420 liters of capacity for luggage and gear.

Safety Features

Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags for comprehensive protection.

ABS: Anti-lock Braking System for enhanced braking control.

EBD: Electronic Brakeforce Distribution for optimized braking performance.

Lane Assist: Lane Keeping Assist to prevent unintended lane departure.

Collision Provision: Active Brake Assist and Forward Collision Warning for added safety.

Parking Sensors: Front and rear sensors for easier parking.

Interior Features

Infotainment System: 10.25-inch touchscreen display with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility.

Climate Control: Dual-zone automatic air conditioning for personalized comfort.

Seating: 5 seats - Premium Artico leather upholstery with power-adjustable seats and memory function for the driver.

Storage: Practical interior storage compartments, cup holders, glovebox with additional space, and rear seats fold down for additional storage space.

Exterior Features

Lighting: LED headlights and taillights for improved visibility and modern aesthetics.

Wheels: 18-inch alloy wheels with a sleek, elegant design.
