Lane Assist: Lane Keeping Assist to help prevent unintended lane departure.

Parking Sensors: Front and rear sensors for parking convenience.

Storage: Ample boot space with a split-folding rear seat for extra storage capacity. Generous interior storage with cup holders, center console, and rear compartments for convenience.
