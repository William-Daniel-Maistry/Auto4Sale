Vehicle Overview

Model Name: BMW M3 Competition

Year: 2024

Brand: BMW

Type: Sedan

Price: R2 100 000

Key Features and Specifications

Engine: 3.0-liter inline-6 twin-turbocharged petrol engine

Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic

Fuel Efficiency: Estimated 10.5 L/100 km (combined) providing a balance between exhilarating performance and reasonable fuel economy

Drive Type: M xDrive all-wheel drive ensures superior traction and control, even in challenging conditions.

Cargo Space: 480 liters

Safety Features

Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags to provide top-notch protection for all occupants.

ABS: Anti-lock Braking System for stable and controlled braking performance.

EBD: Electronic Brakeforce Distribution for enhanced braking control.

Lane Assist: Lane Departure Warning with Lane Keeping Assist to help keep you on track.

Adaptive Cruise Control: Automatically adjusts speed to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead.

360-Degree Camera System: Comprehensive view of your surroundings for added safety during parking and tight maneuvers.

Interior Features

Infotainment System: 12.3-inch BMW iDrive 8.0 display with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, navigation, and real-time traffic information for seamless connectivity.

Climate Control: Multi-zone automatic climate control with fine-tuned settings for maximum comfort.

Seating: 5 seats (2 front, 3 rear) - Merino leather upholstery , with heated and ventilated power-adjustable seats offering lumbar support and memory functions for the ultimate comfort.

Storage: Practical storage compartments, including 2 glove compartments and rear seats fold down for extra space.

Exterior Features

Lighting: Adaptive LED headlights with BMW's signature daytime running lights, providing superior visibility and a striking front appearance.

Wheels: 19-inch front and 20-inch rear alloy wheels , designed for performance and style, complementing the M3's sporty edge.

Panoramic Sunroof: Available for an open, airy feel that enhances the overall driving experience.

Carbon Fiber Roof: Standard on the M3 Competition, reducing weight and enhancing the car's handling dynamics.

M Sport Exhaust: Dual exhaust system with a distinctive, sporty sound that matches the car's high-performance nature.
