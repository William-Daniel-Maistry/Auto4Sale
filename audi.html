Vehicle Overview

Model Name: Audi SQ8 4.0 TFSI Quattro

Year: 2024

Brand: Audi

Type: SUV

Price: R1 800 000

Key Features and Specifications

Engine: 4.0-liter V8 twin-turbocharged petrol engine

Transmission: 8-speed Tiptronic automatic

Fuel Efficiency: 16.8 - 19.6 L/100 km (city), 10.7 - 11.8 L/100 km (highway)

Drive Type: Quattro all-wheel drive (AWD) ensures superior grip and handling, no matter the road conditions.

Cargo Space: Spacious boot for luggage or gear, offering practicality without compromising the vehicle's performance-focused design (605 liters)

Safety Features

Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags for enhanced safety in all conditions.

ABS: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) for superior braking control.

EBD: Electronic Brakeforce Distribution ensures optimal braking efficiency across all surfaces.

Lane Assist: Lane Keeping Assist helps prevent unintentional lane departures, promoting safer driving.

Adaptive Cruise Control: Maintains a safe distance from the vehicle ahead and adjusts speed automatically.

360-Degree Camera System: Offers a comprehensive view of the surroundings to make parking and maneuvering easier.

Interior Features

Infotainment System: Dual 10.1-inch touchscreen displays with Audi MMI Navigation Plus , Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto for seamless connectivity and entertainment.

Climate Control: Multi-zone climate control for personalized comfort for both the driver and passengers.

Seating: 5 seats (2 front, 3 rear)

Storage: Ample interior storage with cup holders, a glovebox, and practical compartments throughout.

Exterior Features

Lighting: Dynamic LED Matrix headlights with Audi's signature daytime running lights for both aesthetic appeal and superior visibility.

Wheels: 21-inch alloy wheels with a bold and stylish design to complement the car's performance-driven nature.

Panoramic Sunroof: Available for an open, airy cabin experience, enhancing the luxury feel.

Tow Bar: Integrated tow bar for carrying light trailers or recreational equipment.
