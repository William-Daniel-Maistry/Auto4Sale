Vehicle Overview

Model Name: Volkswagen Polo GTI

Year: 2024

Brand: Volkswagen

Type: Hatchback

Price: R450 000

Key Features and Specifications

Engine: 2.0-liter 4-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine delivering approximately 200 hp and 320 Nm of torque, offering an exciting performance on both city streets and highways.

Transmission: 7-speed DSG dual-clutch automatic transmission for quick, responsive gear shifts.

Fuel Efficiency: 6.0 liters per 100 km, ensuring a balance between performance and fuel economy.

Drive Type: Front-wheel drive (FWD), delivering nimble handling and a sport-focused driving experience.

Cargo Space: Spacious boot space with practical storage for everyday use or weekend getaways, with rear seats that fold down for extra storage.

Safety Features

Airbags: Front, side, and curtain airbags for enhanced protection in case of a collision.

ABS: Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) ensures controlled braking in all conditions.

EBD: Electronic Brakeforce Distribution ensures optimal braking performance.

Lane Assist: Lane Keeping Assist helps prevent unintended lane departures for safer driving.

Park Distance Control: Rear parking sensors for added convenience while parking in tight spots.

Adaptive Cruise Control: Keeps a safe distance from the vehicle ahead and adjusts speed automatically.

Interior Features

Infotainment System: 8-inch touchscreen display with Volkswagen's Discover Media system, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and navigation for seamless connectivity and entertainment.

Climate Control: Dual-zone automatic climate control to keep both the driver and passengers comfortable.

Seating: 5 seats - Comfortable fabric upholstery.

Sound System: 8-speaker sound system for great audio quality during your drives.

Storage: Practical storage options with cup holders, a center console, and a spacious glovebox.

Exterior Features

Lighting: Full LED headlights and taillights, with signature LED daytime running lights for both visibility and style.

Wheels: 17-inch alloy wheels with a sporty design that complements the Polo GTI's performance-focused look.

Sporty Grille: Signature honeycomb grille and red accents, giving the Polo GTI its iconic sporty look.
